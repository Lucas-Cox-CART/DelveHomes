Amenities

Product Delivery

Products are dropped directly into the homespace, being completely decontaminated and checked for safety before entering.

Airships

To visit other sectors, one may open a tunnel with a button specified in the Book and ride in the car within it to the lift station, in which resides the airships.

The airships themselves are vast, without most of the technologies of the rooms. The furniture and decorations are less advanced, and instead of the buttons one will have a human assistant onboard the ships. There is a revolving platform which lowers guests to their individual rooms, with glass valves marking entryways.

Surface Access

When one wishes to visit the surface, they may apply for an Egression permit and proceed through to the vomitories from which one can view the world.
